Review Rules & Dress Code

Compliance with all regulations is mandatory for entry.

Dress Code Standards

Strictly prohibited attire:

  • Revealing or provocative clothing
  • Halter tops, crop tops, backless tops, sleeveless garments
  • Miniskirts or skirts more than 2 inches above the knee
  • Clothing resembling inmate uniforms (khaki / green military-style)
  • Hats, caps, headwraps
  • See-through garments of any type

Caps / Headwear strictly forbidden

Miniskirts / above knee → not permitted

Reference images: caps.jpg and mini.png. Violations lead to denied entry.

Eligible Visitors

Immediate Family: Mother, Father, Step-parents, Foster parents, Siblings, Spouse, Children

Relatives: Grandparents, Uncles, Aunts, In-laws, Cousins

Others (max 10 associates): Clergy, Civic group members, Attorneys, Sponsors, Parole advisors, Foreign officials.

Visiting Days & Hours

Visiting ends at 4:00 PM. Arrive any time before closure on scheduled day.

  • Upper Section: Mondays & Thursdays
  • Lower Section: Tuesdays & Fridays
  • Remand: Wednesdays & Saturdays

Booking Reference Policy

  • Single-use reference (valid for one entry).
  • Expires at check-out or midnight on visit date.
  • New booking required for each visit.
