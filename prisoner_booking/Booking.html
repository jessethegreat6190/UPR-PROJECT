📋 Review Rules & Dress Code

Please read all rules carefully. You must comply to be allowed entry.

👕 Dress Code

You must wear appropriate, modest clothing. The following are NOT permitted:

  • Revealing or provocative clothing
  • Halter tops, crop tops, backless tops, sleeveless garments
  • Miniskirts or skirts more than 2 inches above the knee
  • Clothing resembling inmate uniforms (khaki or green military-style)
  • Hats or caps
  • See-through garments of any type

👥 Who Can Visit

Immediate Family: Mother, Father, Step-parents, Foster parents, Brothers, Sisters, Spouse, Children

Relatives: Grandparents, Uncles, Aunts, In-laws, Cousins

Others (up to 10 friends/associates): Clergy, Civic group members, Attorneys, Former/prospective employers, Sponsors, Parole advisors, Foreign officials.

🕐 Visiting Days & Hours

Visiting hours end at 4:00 PM. You may arrive any time before 4:00 PM on your scheduled day.

Day restrictions by inmate class:

  • Upper Section: Mondays & Thursdays
  • Lower Section: Tuesdays & Fridays
  • Remand: Wednesdays & Saturdays

⚠️ Booking Reference Rules

  • Your booking reference is single-use only.
  • It expires on check-out or midnight on your booking date.
  • You need a new booking for each visit.
