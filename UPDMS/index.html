About Us

About Uganda Prisons Service

Established in 1909, Uganda Prisons Service is a government agency mandated to keep safe custody of inmates and rehabilitation.

🦅 Our Mission

To provide safe custody of inmates in secure, humane and productive facilities that promote reformation and rehabilitation, thereby contributing to the maintenance of law and order and protection of society.

🎯 Our Vision

A modern, professional, and well-resourced prisons service that ensures public safety through effective custody, reformation, and reintegration of offenders.

⭐ Core Values

🔒 Security

Safe custody of all inmates

⚖️ Justice

Fair and equal treatment
